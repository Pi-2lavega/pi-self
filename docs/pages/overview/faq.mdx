# FAQ

## General

### What is Fira?

Fira is a lending and borrowing protocol specialized in facilitating permissionless fixed-rate markets onchain.

### Why Fira?

The platform addresses a major limitation of decentralized credit: the absence of predictable fixed rates. Fira introduces fixed-rate, maturity-based borrowing and lending as a native onchain primitive, enabling better long-term financial planning.

### Who founded Fira?

Fira was initiated by Usual Lab, a team of experienced professionals in finance, technology, and product development.

### How does Fira work?

Currently, the platform offers Usual Zero Rate (UZR). Fira v1 will be expanding its products to offer fixed-rate markets, floating-rate markets, and dynamic lending & yield trading.

### Which blockchain(s) is Fira deployed on?

Fira will initially be deployed on the Ethereum mainnet, with potential for future deployments on additional blockchains.

### When does Fira V1 release?

V1 is planned to go live by the end of Q1 2026.

## Products

### What products does Fira offer?

Currently: Usual Zero Rate (UZR). Coming with v1: fixed-rate markets, floating-rate markets, and dynamic yield trading.

### How does Fira facilitate fixed-rate borrowing and lending?

Fixed-rate borrowing and lending is made possible by tradable zero-coupon bonds.

### Does Fira offer anything other than fixed-rate borrowing and lending?

Yes. V1 will also add floating markets and traceable yield trading.

### How do I migrate my USL position to the UZR on Fira?

A dedicated USL to UZR migrator smart contract has been launched by Fira.

### How do I borrow on the UZR?

The Usual Zero Rate (UZR) only accepts Bonded USD0 (bUSD0) from Usual as a collateral to borrow underlying USD0 collateral.

### How do I exit the UZR?

Users must repay their USD0 debt to recover collateral that is progressively unlocked.

### Can I lend on the UZR?

No. The Usual DAO sets the lending cap and is the only lender/supplier for the vault.

## Tech & Security

### How is the security of my assets ensured?

Assets are securely stored in audited smart contracts, protected by advanced security measures.

### Is Fira audited?

Yes, all contracts have been audited by independent auditors:
- Sherlock (November 2025)
- Spearbit Cantina (November 2025)
- yAudit (December 2025)
