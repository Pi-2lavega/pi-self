# Product Roadmap

## First Milestone

The launch of **Usual Zero Rate (UZR) on Fira** marks the first step in the full protocol deployment.

## Upcoming Features

### Fixed-Rate Markets

Enables borrowing and lending at determined rates using zero-coupon bond mechanics, providing pricing transparency based on maturities.

### Floating-Rate Markets

Offers variable rate lending and borrowing mechanisms based on utilization, designed for users seeking flexible rates and permanent liquidity.

### Dynamic Lending

Intelligent liquidity allocation between fixed and floating markets to improve efficiency and reinforce segments with the highest demand.

### Yield Trading

Composable yield tokens tied to fixed and floating rates, allowing users to directly trade yield and create structured strategies.
