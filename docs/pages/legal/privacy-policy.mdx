# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** 01/14/2026

## 1. Identity

**ADDU** is a **French association (loi 1901)** governed by French law, based at **10 rue de la Paix, 75002 Paris, France**.

ADDU operates websites and interfaces to access functionalities related to digital assets:
- fira.money
- app.fira.money

ADDU has developed a protocol (the "**Fira Protocol**") enabling interaction with smart contracts (lending/borrowing, staking, transactions).

## 2. Purpose

This policy explains:
- Personal data collected
- Legal bases for processing
- Data recipients
- Retention periods
- GDPR rights

## 3. Definitions

- **Personal data**: any information identifying a person (email, IP address, wallet address)
- **Processing**: operation on data (collection, storage, deletion)
- **Controller**: ADDU, except for blockchain data

## 4. Minors

Services are not intended for minors (under 18 years old). Any personal data from children will be promptly deleted.

### 5.2 Technical Data

## 6. Processing Purposes and Legal Bases

### 6.1 Purpose Table

**A) Support and Communications**
- Data: name, email, content
- Purposes: respond to requests, user support
- Bases: contract execution, legitimate interests, consent

**B) Service Provision and Improvement**
- Data: technical data, logs, events
- Purposes: operate site, fix bugs, improve UX
- Bases: legitimate interests, contract execution

**C) Security and Fraud Prevention**
- Data: logs, IP, technical signals
- Purposes: secure site, detect attacks
- Bases: legitimate interests, legal obligations

**D) Legal Compliance**
- Data: context-dependent
- Purposes: respond to authorities
- Bases: legal obligation

**E) Non-Essential Cookies**
- Data: identifiers, audience measurement
- Purposes: analytics, personalization
- Bases: consent

### 6.2 Mandatory vs Optional Data

Some data (security logs, technical cookies) are **required** for operation. Support data is **optional**.

Data may be processed outside EEA. Safeguards applied:

- Additional measures (encryption, minimization, access control)

## 9. Retention Period

Retention "as long as necessary", then deletion/anonymization.

Indicative periods:
- **Support/communications**: up to 5 years archiving
- **Account/relationship data**: deletion 5 years after closure
- **Transaction data**: 5 years from transaction
- **Security logs**: a few months (except incidents)

**Note**: data recorded on blockchain is non-erasable.

## 10. Blockchain-Specific Notice

Public blockchains record transactions **permanently**.

- ADDU **does not control** blockchains (decentralized networks)
- Rights to erasure and rectification are **technically limited** for on-chain data
- Recommendation: do not insert directly identifying data in public fields

## 11. Cookies and Similar Technologies

### 11.1 Definition
File/identifier stored on device for technical functionality, audience measurement, preferences.

### 11.2 Strictly Necessary Cookies
Essential for site operation (session, security, load balancing) – **no consent required**.

### 11.3 Non-Essential Cookies
Analytics, personalization – **consent required**. Modifiable via cookie banner.

### 11.4 Cookie List
Available via preference center (name, purpose, retention, provider).

## 12. Security

Reasonable technical and organizational measures: access control, minimization, logging, backups, application security.

No method guarantees absolute security.

## 13. GDPR Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access** your data
- **Rectify** your data
- **Erase** your data (certain cases)
- **Restrict** processing
- **Object** (particularly legitimate interests)
- **Data portability**
- **Withdraw consent** (without retroactive effect)
- **Post-mortem instructions** (French law)

**Blockchain limitation**: certain rights are limited for on-chain data.
